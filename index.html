What is anxiety? Have you experienced it?

Anxiety is a natural response to stress, characterized by feelings of worry, fear, and unease. It can manifest physically and mentally, impacting daily functioning. Persistent or excessive anxiety may indicate an anxiety disorder, requiring professional intervention for effective management.
